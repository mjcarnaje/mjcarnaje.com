## Visualize Your Legacy: Build Your Interactive Family Tree

Family Nodes is a comprehensive platform designed to help you connect your past, present, and future. It addresses every aspect of preserving and exploring your family history through a modern, responsive interface.

### Core Features

- **Interactive Node-Based Visualization** - Node-based interface with zoom, pan, and auto-layout that automatically organizes complex relationships.
- **Member Management** - Detailed profiles with life events, media galleries, and notes.
- **Collaborative Editing** - Real-time collaboration with role-based permissions.
- **Tree Sharing** - Public or private trees with secure invitations.
- **Relationship Tracking** - Parent-child relationships, marriages, and sibling connections.
- **Media Management** - Upload and organize photos and documents.
- **Genealogy Import** - Import GEDCOM files to quickly build your tree.
- **Search & Discovery** - Full-text search across family members and events.

### Tech Stack

- **Framework**: TanStack Start (Full-stack React framework)
- **Database**: PostgreSQL with Drizzle ORM
- **Authentication**: Better Auth
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS with Radix UI
- **File Storage**: Cloudinary

### The Mission

The goal of Family Nodes is to make genealogy accessible and engaging. Instead of just names on a chart, it's a living history where you can discover how families are using it to preserve their heritage and connect with their roots.

Built by **mj carnaje**, Family Nodes is completely free, allowing families around the world to build a lasting record of their heritage without any barriers.
